Banerjee Law is a U.S. immigration and nationality law firm located in the San Francisco Bay Area. We specialize in employment-based immigration law and work with companies to strategize on the best work visa options and immigration pathways to help them hire and retain highly skilled foreign nationals. We are experienced in helping startup founders and entrepreneurs from around the world, executives and managers, scientists and professionals secure U.S. work authorization, permanent residence, and citizenship.

Shilpi Banerjee, Esq., is the Founder and Principal Attorney at Banerjee Law. Prior to founding Banerjee Law, Ms. Banerjee worked for 8 years in a boutique immigration law firm in the San Francisco Bay Area specializing in business immigration. She has helped secure U.S. visas and permanent residence for founders, scientists, executives and managers, and high skill employees at companies in a variety of industries.

As an immigrant and a first-generation American who moved to the United States from her hometown in New Delhi, India, Ms. Banerjee views immigration law not just as a profession, but as a personal journey. Having been through the immigration process herself, she empathizes with our clients and strives to guide them through the intricacies of the U.S. immigration system.

Ms. Banerjee earned a J.D. (Juris Doctor) degree from the University of Colorado School of Law in Boulder, Colorado, where she served as a Member & Editor of the University of Colorado Law Review and worked as a Judicial Fellow at the Colorado Supreme Court. She has a Master’s degree in Economics from the Delhi School of Economics (India) and a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from Lady Shri Ram College (New Delhi, India).

Ms. Banerjee (connect on LinkedIn) is a member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA) and is admitted to practice law in California and Colorado. For inquiries about U.S. visas and immigration, please reach out to us at shilpi@bimmigrationlaw.com
